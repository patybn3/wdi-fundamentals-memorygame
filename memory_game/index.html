A Memory Game!

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.




Instructions

Remembrance is an easy game to learn. All you have to do is click on a card to flip it, remember it, and click on a second card to find out if it is a match. You want to match the Queen of Hearts to the Queen of Diamonds or the King of Hearts to the King of Diamonds.
Do not worry, if you don't get it right the first time, you can try again as many times as you need. Just click on "TRY AGAIN" to restart.

What are you waiting for? Click on the cards to find a match!

Why Remembrance?

Playing this game daily can sharpen your memorization skills in addition to giving you several hours of fun! This game is addictive but it will not waste your time since it has been proven to be an enthusiastic and productive way to train your brain.
